- nosh-server is Unix-only (portable-pty, nix) — MUST NOT be built on Windows; use `-p nosh-client` (nosh-auth compiles transitively, its #[cfg(unix)] gates are in place).
- `Swatinem/rust-cache@v2` is the standard Rust caching action [A1: ASSUMED but widely used].
