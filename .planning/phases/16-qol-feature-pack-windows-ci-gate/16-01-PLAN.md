| T-16-02 | Denial of Service | osc_dispatch OSC accumulation (vte std re-enabled) | mitigate | Explicit caps `OSC_52_MAX_BYTES=65536`, `MAX_TITLE_BYTES=1024` in osc_dispatch; transient vte buffer bounded by OS pipe buffer. | T-16-03 | Tampering | OSC 0/2 title forwarded to client terminal | accept | Title is emitted by the local terminal as window-title text, not interpreted as markup/cells; no injection surface. Bounded to MAX_TITLE_BYTES. |
