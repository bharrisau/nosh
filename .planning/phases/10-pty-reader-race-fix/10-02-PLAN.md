| orphaned reader → reattach reader clone | The prior reader must be fully exited before reattach clones a fresh reader, or two threads read the same master fd and split bytes non-deterministically. |

| T-10-04 | Information Disclosure / Tampering | reattach reader-clone race (Pitfall 3) | mitigate | TransportLost arm awaits the reader's clean exit (`signal_shutdown` + bounded join timeout) BEFORE `registry.orphan(&slot)`; a later reattach therefore clones a reader only after the prior reader is gone — no two live readers, no garbled/split output. Verified: reader await precedes `registry.orphan` in both arms. |
